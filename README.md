# 📊 Hotel Revenue Analysis Project

## 📌 Overview
This project analyzes hotel performance data to uncover insights into revenue trends, customer behavior, and operational efficiency.

The goal is to provide data-driven recommendations that improve profitability and customer experience.

---

## 📂 Dataset
The dataset includes:
- Booking information
- Customer demographics
- Revenue data
- Room types
- Booking channels

---

## 🛠 Tools Used
- Power BI
- Excel
- Data Cleaning & Transformation

---

## 📊 Key Insights

### 🔹 Revenue Trends
- Revenue peaked in April (GHC 2.07M)
- Significant drop observed in July (GHC 837K)
- Indicates strong seasonality in bookings

### 🔹 Channel Performance
- Online Travel Agencies generated the highest revenue (GHC 2.95M)
- Shows importance of digital platforms

### 🔹 Customer Behavior
- Most preferred room type: Double rooms
- Highest bookings from males aged 46–55

### 🔹 Booking Patterns
- Peak bookings in March
- Decline observed towards year-end

### 🔹 Operational Insights
- Cancellation rate of 19% indicates improvement opportunities

---

## 💡 Recommendations

- Increase investment in digital marketing
- Introduce seasonal promotions during low-demand periods
- Personalize customer experience using demographic data
- Improve cancellation policies to reduce revenue loss
- Enhance premium service offerings
